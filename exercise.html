Benefits of Exercise

Regular physical activity is essential for maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Here are some benefits:

  • Improves mental health and mood.
  • Increases energy levels.
  • Helps with weight management.
  • Reduces the risk of chronic diseases.
  • Enhances sleep quality.

Types of Exercise

  • Aerobic Exercise: Running, cycling, swimming.
  • Strength Training: Weight lifting, resistance exercises.
  • Flexibility Exercises: Yoga, stretching.
